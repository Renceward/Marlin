Halloween.

Our not very scary Halloween show runs from early October through Halloween night. Enjoy a playlist that with songs for all ages including everything from Purple People Eater to The Phantom of the Opera with Ghostbusters and Monster Mash thrown in for good measure. Our playlist changes from night to night throughout the Halloween season.

Christmas

Our very merry Christmas Show runs from shortly after Thanksgiving through New Years Eve. Songs include traditional holiday classics as well as contemporary favorites. Just like with Halloween, our playlist changes nightly throughout the holiday season.
